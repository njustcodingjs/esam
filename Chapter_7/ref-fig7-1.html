Fig. 7-1.  Contour maps of the molecular charge distributions of N2 and LiF at their equilibrium internuclear separations. The space to the right of the dashed line through the Li nucleus denotes the region of nonbonded charge density. The values of the contours increase from the outermost one to the innermost one.
